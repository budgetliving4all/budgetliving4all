## Frequently asked questions

FAQ sections often get picked up as Google featured snippets. Write 3–5 questions your target reader would actually search for.

### How much does it cost to meal prep for one person per week?

Answer in 2–4 sentences. Be specific. Example: *Meal prepping for one person typically costs $25–$50 per week depending on your protein choices and where you shop. Buying staples like rice, oats, and lentils in bulk, and choosing cheaper protein sources like eggs, canned tuna, or chicken thighs keeps costs at the lower end.*

### How long does meal-prepped food last in the fridge?

Most meal-prepped dishes last 4–5 days in the fridge when stored in airtight containers. Cooked grains and roasted vegetables stay fresh for up to 5 days; cooked proteins are best consumed within 3–4 days. For longer storage, freeze individual portions.
